About Us

Rent a Runt is an organisation that links YOU to animals. The organisation has many functions and goals, but the principle is that you are able to rent an animal of your choice for a given time.
  • Helps you understand if you are ready for a pet
  • If owners have to go away and have no one to leave their animal with- it helps them to find shelter and helps you to experience having a pet.
  • You can spend time with animals from shelters. Make them and you happy. Maybe you will find your 'the one'
